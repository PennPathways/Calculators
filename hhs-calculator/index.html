After HHS Resolved: Routine Inpatient Diabetes Care
  • POC glucose QAc & QHs if eating; Q4–6h if NPO/TPN/TEN
  • Order prandial insulin and correctional (sliding scale) insulin
  • Consider 3AM fingerstick night of transition to evaluate basal insulin effect
  • Ensure daily basal insulin ordered within 24h of NOW basal dose
  • Watch for decreasing insulin requirements as precipitants and glucose toxicity resolve
IV → SQ Insulin Transition
  • Administer glargine before stopping IV infusion (IV insulin t½ = 10–15 min)
  • Administer glargine as soon as delivered from Pharmacy once HHS is resolved
  • Discontinue insulin + dextrose infusions 2 hours after glargine given
  • If restarting home pump: discontinue IV infusions 1 hour after pump restarted
  • Consult diabetes/endocrine for patients on home insulin pump (if available)
